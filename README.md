
# 🚖 Uber Trip Analytics

An end-to-end Business Intelligence project built using **Power BI** to analyze Uber trip data and transform raw trip records into actionable business insights.

This project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow including **data cleaning, feature engineering, KPI creation, interactive dashboard development, and business storytelling**.

---

# 📌 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze Uber trip data and answer key business questions that can help stakeholders understand customer travel behavior, demand patterns, and operational performance.

Instead of creating random charts, the dashboard was designed around real business questions that decision-makers would ask.

---

# ❓ Business Questions Answered

### 🚗 Overall Service Usage
- How many trips were completed?
- How many total miles were travelled?
- What is the average trip distance?
- What is the average trip duration?

---

### 📅 Demand Analysis
- Which months have the highest number of trips?
- Which weekdays experience the highest demand?
- At what hour of the day is Uber used the most?

---

### 👥 Customer Segmentation
- What percentage of trips are Business trips?
- What percentage of trips are Personal trips?

---

### 📍 Location Analysis
- Which pickup locations generate the highest demand?
- Which destinations receive the highest number of trips?
- Which travel routes are most frequently used?

---

### 📊 Operational Insights
- What are the peak travel hours?
- How does travel demand change throughout the year?
- How does trip behavior vary across weekdays?

---
